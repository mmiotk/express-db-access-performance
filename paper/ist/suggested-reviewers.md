Pick 3–5 for the Editorial Manager form, in this order:

1. **Tse-Hsun (Peter) Chen** — Associate Professor, Computer Science & Software

   performance (TOSEM 2024, ICSE/ASE 2025).

   https://www.fim.uni-passau.de/en/database-systems
