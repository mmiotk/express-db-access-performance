\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We set out to replace vendor-benchmark folklore with a neutral, reproducible
comparison of relational database access layers in Express.js. Our harness covers
the full taxonomy---native drivers, a query builder, and six ORMs---across
PostgreSQL and MySQL, over five access patterns, reporting throughput and tail
latency together. This closes four gaps left by prior art: the absence of MySQL,
the lack of a neutral full-taxonomy study, the separation of throughput from tail
latency, and the omission of the Express HTTP layer.

\emph{Quantitative findings and guidance will follow the first measurement run.}
Planned extensions include a connection-pool-size sweep, an isolated N+1 penalty
study, and cold-start latency per layer. The complete replication package---harness,
seed, and analysis---is released under open licenses.
