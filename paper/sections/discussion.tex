\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

\emph{To be written after results.} Intended threads:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item Where abstraction cost is negligible (e.g.\ point reads dominated by
        network/parse) versus where it compounds (deep fetch, aggregation).
  \item Whether the throughput ranking and the tail-latency ranking agree---if a
        layer wins on average but loses on p99, that is the paper's practical punch.
  \item PostgreSQL vs MySQL: does engine choice reorder the layers?
  \item Practical guidance: when the ergonomics of an ORM are worth the measured
        cost, and when a thin layer is warranted.
\end{itemize}
